Quesadilla with Bacon and Veggies

Pictured above, a quesadilla with bacon and veggies. A simple and easy dish that can be made in a pinch!

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Tortillas
  • 2 types of cheese(mozzarella & pepper jack?)
  • Tomatoes
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Get strips of bacon and chop into chunks and put into the pan, let cook
  2. Cut tomatoes and cheese
  3. Tilt the pan to fry the bacon in its own fat
  4. When bacon is finished, reserve the extra fat(still leave some in the pan)
  5. Throw in some garlic and cook for a bit(and optionally onions)
  6. Put spinach in pan and let it wilt down and cook
  7. Assemble quesadilla
    1. Tortilla, then layer with mozzarella, then bacon, then spinach, then tomatoes, and then the pepper jack cheese
    2. Fold and smush down
  8. Use saved bacon grease to fry quesadilla
  9. Put quesadilla on, then quickly after flip to other side to make sure both sides are evenly distributed with the bacon grease
  10. Fry until melted and golden brown(clean while doing this)
  11. Cut and serve
