Adobo

Pictured above, chicken adobo, the national dish of the Philppines.

Ingredients

  • meat(pork rib, chicken, beef chuck)
  • garlic(whole)
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • sugar(optional)
  • bay leaf
  • peppercorn

Steps

  1. wash meat, cut the meat in between the bones
  2. make marinade
  3. 2 bay leaves, 1:1 soy sauce to vinegar (1/2cup for a full instant pot), a little bit of sugar, 2 pinches of peppercorn, whole garlic (crush, dice), mix together
  4. add meat to the marinade(in a bag)
  5. leave overnight
  6. add all contents to instant pot
  7. make sure valve is on sealing
  8. press manual, 18mins(for pork)
  9. let cook
  10. flip valve to venting
  11. when metal indicator goes down, then you can open it
  12. open instant pot
  13. serve
  14. remove the the scum with fat skimmer
