Risotto

Pictured above, a finshed plate of risotto garnished with green onions. Risotto is a traditional Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice
  • Mushrooms (24 ounces)
  • Garlic
  • Onions/Shallots
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • White Wine (Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Grigio)
  • Parmegiano Reggiano

Steps

  1. brush off/ clean mushrooms
  2. finely dice 2 shallots (or 2 shallots worth of onions)s
  3. finely dice 2-3 cloves of garlics
  4. add 1 tbsp of oil and 2 tbsp of butter to pots
  5. set instant pot to sauté settings
  6. once melted, add shallots/onions to pot and sauté for approx 2 minss
  7. add garlic to pot, stir for 1 mins
  8. add sliced mushrooms to pots
  9. add another 2 tbsp of butters
  10. stir everything around (get it homogenized) and make sure bottom of the pot is clean (approx 3 mins)s
  11. add 1/2 cup of a dry white wine, stir and let sit for 1 mins
  12. add in 2 cups of arborio rice, stirs
  13. add in 4.5 cups of chicken stock, stir and make sure that anything that was stuck to the bottom of the pot is lifteds
  14. add 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1/4 tsp of italian seasoning, stir (can just eyeball this)s
  15. close instant pot, press cancels
  16. cook manually for 6 mins on high pressures
  17. once finished, quick release and stirs
  18. then add 1/2 cup of parmigiano reggiano, stirs
  19. serve with fresh cracks of black pepper and top with cheeses
